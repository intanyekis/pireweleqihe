    Tom Brady &amp; Gisele Renegotiate Prenup After His $375 Million Fox Deal

    Tom Brady and Gisele Bundchen have been married since 2009, but only now is the supermodel reportedly re-looking at their prenuptial agreement and the timing comes right after Tom landed a multi-million-dollar deal with Fox Sports.

    24 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Tom Brady and Gisele Bundchen have been married since 2009, but only now is the supermodel reportedly re-looking at their prenuptial agreement – and the timing comes right after Tom landed a multi-million-dollar deal with Fox Sports.

    According to Suggest, a source claims that Gisele’s friends have been pushing her to look over the prenup to ensure she gets a fair share of her husband’s income should they separate.

    RELATED: Johnny Depp’s 'Cry Baby' Harley Can Be Yours For Six Figures

    “Gisele is rich in her own right,” the insider said. “But her friends are telling her she’d be nuts not to revise their prenup and she’s likely to listen to them.”

    “The current prenup is pretty ironclad and lists how everything will be divided should they break up, but it needs to be updated and it can be if both parties agree,” they continued.

    In May, it was announced that Tom had signed a record-breaking deal to join Fox Sports as its lead NFL analyst following his retirement. The athlete was offered $375 million for the gig – which surpasses the $333 million he’s made during his two-decade career in the NFL, Forbes points out.

    Despite saying he was retiring, Tom only last 40 days following the announcement before revealing he would be returning to the field.

    The NFL star previously spent 20 seasons with the New England Patriots, and later gave 2 seasons to the Tampa Bay Buccaneers, which is the team the quarterback has since returned to.

    Tom and Gisele began dating in 2006 after being set up on a blind date. They later wed in 2009 during a Catholic ceremony held in Santa Monica. They have two children – a son, Benjamin, born in 2009, and a daughter, Vivian, born in 2012.

    The couple often decline to share details of their relationship, though there’s been much media speculation about their marriage over the years.

    For instance, in 2020, it was reported that pair were “living separate lives” after they “drifted apart. “[Tom and Gisele] realized they’re two very different people. He likes a structured life and she’s all over the place — he’ll come home to a note saying she’s gone to Costa Rica to meditate!” a source said.

    A previous quote from Tom in which he said “I’m going one way and she’s going in the other” furthered public speculation the pair were on the rocks.

    However, Tom and Gisele still appear happily married, and the model seems supportive of her husband’s big career accomplishments.
