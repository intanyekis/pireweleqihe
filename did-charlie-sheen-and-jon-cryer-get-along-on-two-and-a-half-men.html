    Charlie Sheen and Jon Cryer famously portrayed Charlie and Alan Harper on the hilarious CBS show, Two and A Half Men. The series, which first began in 2003, officially ended in 2015 after 12 successful seasons. While most folks tuned in for the over-the-top funny rapport between on-screen brothers, Charlie and Alan, things took a major turn in 2011. Charlie Sheen was fired from the show after exhibiting "dangerously self-destructive conduct", which completely shifted the overall environment behind the scenes.

    Although fans didn't know what producers would do with the show moving forward, they managed to find a solution and brought on Ashton Kutcher to complete the final four seasons.

    Since the show's conclussion, Jon Cryer has come forward to talk about his time on the show with Charlie and whether the two ever really got along or put on an act for the cameras. Let's find out!

    Charlie Sheen Was Fired From Two And A Half Men For His Behavior

    After spending eight years working together on the set of Two And A Half Men, Jon Cryer opened up about his experience with Charlie Sheen.     Jon Cryer And Charlie Sheen Remained Friends After Two And A Half Men

    Jon Cryer had nothing but good things to say about Charlie Sheen a few years after Two and a Half Men ended

    Jon Cryer spoke with People TV and revealed that his time with Charlie Sheen was nothing but great! Cryer continued to express how the duo shared nothing but great moments and how he has such fond memories of Sheen's time on the show.

    "Charlie and I really hit it off. We had a great first few years on that show," he said. "It was incredibly smooth, we had a lovely time, it was working really well." Cryer finished.

    While Jon believes that things did take a turn when Charlie resorted to drugs and alcohol, he said that he remained kind and friendly to everyone in the cast and crew. Despite remaining professional, Jon says that things began to wear Charlie down and the time in between Sheen's firing and getting a replacement was "crazy" and an absolute "sh-t storm".

    Although many thought Charlie and Jon had their differences, the duo were, in fact, the best of friends!

    Jon Cryer Does Not Have Any Plans To Work With Charlie Sheen In The Future

    Jon Cryer is hesitant to work with Charlie Sheen in the future

    Even though Jon and Charlie had great on-screen chemistry with one another and fans would love to see them team up for another project, Jon has his reservations about working with Charlie again. This, despite knowing that Charlie is seemingly doing better than he was over a decade ago.

    "Charlie is doing a lot better now, which is wonderful," Jon explained. "He and I have not spoken in a few years, but he's doing a lot better, which obviously I'm very happy about."
