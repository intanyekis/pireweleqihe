    AJK Public Service Commission AJKPSC Jobs 2022 has been announced through Azad Jammu and Kashmir Public Service Commission at the online job portal www.ajkpsc.gov.pk. The official advertisement can be seen below. Applications are invited from Pakistani Nationals having domicile of Azad Jammu & Kashmir for the following posts given below in the advertisement. Interested candidates can apply online at the official website. Those candidates who fulfill the eligibility criteria can apply for these posts. Age, qualification, experience, and domicile prescribed for the posts are mentioned against each in the advertisement given below.
