    6 feet 4 inches tall, Lauren Stivrins is a professional volleyball player for Nebraska as a middle blocker.

    Lauren was named an AVCA second-team All-American after she averages 1.07 blocks per set and 2.55 kills with a .374 hitting percentage in total 33 matches played.

    Lauren has earned AVCA All-North Region and All-Big Ten First-Team honors for the second straight year and was ranked third in the Big Ten with a .412 hitting percentage during conference play.

    She named the Husker Invitational All-Tournament Team after nine kills and two blocks on .500, hitting against No. 18 Creighton and putting up a season-high 18 kills on .361 beating at No. 20 Purdue with a career-high 36 attacks.

    Stivrins joined the Huskers from Chaparral High School in Scottsdale, Arizona, where she ranked the No. 9 prospects in 2016 by PrepVolleyball.com and No. 3 middle blockers and led Chaparral.
