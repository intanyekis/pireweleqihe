    Model and an actress, Elizabeth Hurley, has a son, Damian, with whom she shares a close bond. Damian, on the other hand, is always gushing about his mother, and he calls her names like "twin," "partner in crime," and "favorite human."

    In 1994, Elizabeth Hurley made history when she walked the red carpet at the premiere of "Four Weddings and a Funeral" in a Gianni Versace black safety-pinned gown. The outfit also helped catapult an unknown actress to stardom.

    On June 12, 2022, the "Bedazzled" actress decided to have another Versace moment as she celebrated her birthday in an equally alluring Versace style.

    Damian Charles Hurley and Elizabeth Hurley on the front row of the Versace special event during the Milan Fashion Week - Spring / Summer 2022 on September 26, 2021 | Source: Getty Images

    The actress took to Instagram to post a picture of her wearing a sparkling pink chainmail dress with wild cutouts similar to the black dress she pulled off almost three decades ago.

    In the second slide of her post, Hurley posed with her son, Damian, who also channeled a Versace look. He wore the black Versace Medusa Biggie jacquard blazer jacket and matching black pants.

    ELIZABETH HURLEY'S SON DAMIAN CALLS HER 'BEST FRIENDS/TWIN/FAVORITE HUMAN'

    Damian not only poses in pictures with his mother, but they are so close that he calls her sweet names. The mother and son, who look alike, share a special bond.

    On March 27, 2022, while celebrating the UK's Mother's Day, Damian took to his Instagram to pay tribute to his mother. He never misses an opportunity to let her know how special she is to him.

    For Mother's Day, Damian shared a black-and-white photo of Hurley doting over him as a baby. In the caption, he wrote:

    "Happy mamas' day to best friend/twin/favourite human etc etc."

    DAMIAN'S TRAGIC LOSS OF HIS FATHER FIGURE, SHANE WARNE

    After the death of Australian international Hall of Fame cricketer Shane Warne on March 4, 2022, Hurley and Damian went through a heartbreaking few weeks.

    The star's management revealed that the cricketer was found unresponsive in his villa, and they could not revive him despite the best efforts of the medical staff.

    While Hurley was sad that she could not travel to Australia for the funeral, Damian also paid tribute to him. On Instagram, Damian wrote:

    "I'm still trying to wrap my head around thisâ€¦ SW was a father figure to me for most of my formative years and was truly one of the best men I've ever known. My heart is broken. Thinking of and sending love to all SW's family."

    IS ELIZABETH HURLEY MARRIED?

    Hurley has had her fair share of relationships. In 1987, she met and dated actor Hugh Grant. They were together for 13 years, and despite their split, they are still close, and he is her son's godfather.

    There were rumors that she had an affair with actor and comedian Denis Leary in the early 2000s. However, they both said they were just friends, and he is one of the six godfathers Damian has.

    In 2000, she had a fling with billionaire businessman Theodore Forstmann, but they never commented on it. For 18 months in the early 2000s, Hurley dated businessman, philanthropist, film producer, and screenwriter Steve Bing.

    They had their son in 2002 after their split. After her split from Bing, Hurley dated former NBA player Steve Nash before dating Indian international businessman and entrepreneur Arun Nayar in 2002.

    Hurley met her husband Nayar after London-based financier Alessandro Tome introduced them over dinner in January 2003. They married in 2007 and finalized their divorce in 2011 after separating in 2010.

    Three months after finalizing her divorce from Nayar, she was engaged to Warne, but they split in 2013. In 2019, Hurley sparked romance rumors with actor and filmmaker Justin Theroux, but she said they were not true.
