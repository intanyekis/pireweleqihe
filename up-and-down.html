    Overall Meaning

    In Doja Cat's song Up and Down, the lyrics revolve around the cyclical nature of life, which can take you from feeling high to feeling low, much like a yo-yo going up and down. The song speaks to the resilience needed to survive the constant changes in life, and how we must adapt by going up and down. Doja Cat acknowledges that sometimes she feels shit, and at other times, she feels like she's the shit. Life can get under her skin, much like a tick, and some people make her sick, but she chooses to bite her fists and wet her wrist instead of letting them get to her. She encourages listeners to keep going even when they feel lost or down, and she confidently asserts that she is a "real bitch" who is in charge.


    The lyrics to Up and Down are quite empowering and remind us that even when we feel low, we can always rise again. The song is popular for its catchy beat and infectious energy, but it's the lyrics that make it so relatable to listeners. Overall, Doja Cat's Up and Down speaks to the human experience of navigating life's ups and downs, encouraging us all to keep on pushing through.
