Here are the top 10 richest actors and actresses that starred in Can’t Hardly Wait, ranked in increasing order of net worth, from the rich to the richest.

    Lauren Ambrose - $10 Million

    Lauren Ambrose is an American actress and singer. Lauren played the role of Denise Fleming in Can’t Hardly Wait. She is best known for her work on Broadway. She is the winner of an award for Outstanding Ensemble Performance from both the Actors Guild (which she won in 2003 and 2004) and the Drama Desk Award (which she won in 2016) for her roles in Six Feet Under and Awake and Sing, respectively.

    Donald Faison - $10 Million

    Donald Faison is an actor, voice actor, and comedian. He is popular for his role in Scrubs, and Clueless. The American actor plays the role of Dan in Can’t Hardly Wait. He also had appearances as a co-star in Waiting to Exhale, Remember the Titans, and Next Day Air. The actor was born in Harlem, New York. His first TV appearance was from a 1991 commercial where the then 17-year-old plays the younger brother of a veteran. It was later that he got a major role acting in Clueless and Scrubs. The 45-year-old currently has a net worth of $10 million.

    Robert Jayne - $11 Million

    Robert Jayne is an American actor who plays the role of Richie Colby in the movie Can’t Hardly Wait. The 46-year-old started his acting career at a young age and spent most of his early life on screen. He has appeared in many television series as well as movies. He has appeared in such popular movies as Meet the Appelgates and, Wizards of the Lost Kingdom II. He has also appeared in TV series including, The Love Boat, The Greatest American Hero, and Knots Landing. He currently has a net worth of $11 million. He is the winner of the 1988 Young Artist Award for Best Young Actor.

    Melissa Joan Hart - $14 Million

    The American actress plays the role of Vicki in Can’t Hardly Wait. She is most popular for playing Clarissa in Clarissa Explains It All. She has also starred in popular blockbusters such as Sabrina The Teenage Witch, and God’s Not Dead. Melissa has also produced and directed movies. The 43-year-old has a net worth of $14 million. Joan’s career began at a very young age – when she was only four. She started with a TV commercial and by age 5 she had been in 25 commercials. She also had a guest role in The Equalizer franchise in 1986. She is the winner of 7 awards and has been nominated for 11 others.

    Jenna Elfman - $16 Million

    Jenna Elfman is among the un-credited cast of Can’t Hardly Wait. A lot of fans have been wondering who the angel stripper in the movie is. Well, it’s Jenna here. Elfman is a native of California. She started as a dancer; appearing in four music videos between 1990 and 1994. In 1997 she co-starred in the TV sitcom Dharma and Greg. In 2012, she and her husband started a joined podcast. A year later she appeared in two comedy series. Jenna started having appearances on TV as an actress in 1992. Since then she has been in numerous TV programs and movies and is currently part of the cast of Fear the Walking Dead. She currently has a net worth of $16 million.

    Jennifer Love Hewitt - $18 Million

    She is an American actress and musician. Jennifer plays the role of Amanda Beckett, the girl who all the guys want to be with, in Can’t Hardly Wait. She has also made appearances in the 2001 Heartbreakers (co-starring with the famous Jackie Chan) and the CBS Ghost Whisperer.

    Jerry O'Connell - $20 Million

    Jerry O’Connell is an American actor, director, and talk show host. His show the Jerry O plays on Fox.     Jaime Pressly - $20 Million

    Jaime Pressly is an American model and actress. She is the winner of an Emmy Award for Best Supporting Actress, which she won for her role in the 2005 TV series My Name Is Earl.     Seth Green - $40 Million
