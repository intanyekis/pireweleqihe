    This is to inform you that a tragic plane crash occurred in Tennessee on Sunday evening. Shock waves blew from Reliance, Tennessee where a small plane crashed after three hours departing from South Bend, Indiana. The victim of the plane crash has been identified as Dr. Lionel Meadows. According to FlightAware, the small plane crash that happened on Sunday evening cost the life of Dr. Lionel Meadows. Since this news broke out and surfaced, people have been taking over the internet and sending their heartfelt condolences to his family. But many are also asking what led to the crash or what went wrong before the air mishap. Numerous questions have been making headlines related to this news on social media.     Who was Dr. Lionel Meadows? dies in Plane Crash

    Reportedly, the flight departed from South Bend, Indiana on Sunday afternoon at 4 pm but it could not land safely as the flight crashed in Tennessee. Steve Lofty, the director of Polk County EMA confirmed the news of the fatal plane crash. A victim of the incident was identified as Dr. Lionel Meadows who was a popular physician. Dr. Lionel Meadows was a native of Northeast Georgia. Scroll down the page and read more details.

    The plane crash happened in Polk County near the border of Georgia and Tennessee. More about the crash, the plane involved in the crash was Cessna 182 single-engine plane. A person called 911 to inform the authorities about the plane crash. Reportedly, Dr. Lionel Meadows was flying alone during the accident. He was going to visit his family when the small plane crashed due to a storm on Sunday evening. However, the exact cause of the crash is still yet to be known. But an investigation is going on.

    Meadows Surgical Arts wrote while expressing sorrow on Dr. Lionel Meadows’s death, “Dr. Meadows was a beloved friend, mentor, and inspiration to many of us. He was a brilliant surgeon and doctor who touched the lives of so many with his kindness and passion for delivering exceptional patient care. Dr. Meadows was known for praying with each of his patients before surgery, and we ask for your prayers now as we mourn his death. Our thoughts and prayers are with his family and friends during this difficult time.” Stay tuned to this website for more details and updates.
