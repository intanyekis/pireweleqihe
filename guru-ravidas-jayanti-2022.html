    Guru Ravidass Jayanti News: - "Guru Ravidass Jayanti" is the birthday of Guru Ravidas, celebrating Magh Purnima, the full moon day of Magh. This is the aim of the Ravidassia religion every year. The date of Jayanti in 2020 is February 9 and the date of 2021 is February 16.

    When is Guru Ravidas Jayanti?

    In India, today is a public holiday in Haryana, Himachal Pradesh, and Punjab. It is celebrated throughout the month of Magh.

    History and significance of Guru Ravidas Jayanti

    Guru Ravidas was born in Seer Govardhanpur, Uttar Pradesh, India at the end of the 14th century. He was born into a family rarely considered unaffected.

    Guru Ravidas was one of the leaders in asserting that all Indians should have human rights. He became an important figure in the Bhakti movement, teaching spiritual beliefs and striving to spread the message of equality as freedom from tribal persecution in India.

    41 of his songs and poems are found in the Sikh scriptures, Guru Granth Sahib. A revered figure in Hindu spiritualism, Meera Bai regards Guru Ravidas as her spiritual guru.

    The teachings of the Guru now form the basis of the Ravidassia religion. Ravidassia believed that Guru Ravidas should be considered a saint like any other guru because he lived before the first Sikh guru and his teachings were learned by Sikh gurus. In recent years this has led to conflict with Sikhs and caused Ravidassia to move away from the Sikh model.

    Guru Ravidass Jayanti 2022: Date and Time

    Purnima tithi begins at 9:16 p.m. on February 15, 2022, and ends at 1:25 a.m. on February 16, 2022.

    How is the day marked?

    Today Guru Ravidass followers read Amritbani Guru Ravidas Ji and make a special aarti in his honor. People walk in holy water and pray in temples for saints. The biggest celebration was held in honor of Shri Guru Ravidass Janam Asthan Mandir in Varanasi with Nagar Kirtan as the main attraction. Saints' birthdays, or Jayanti, are particularly celebrated in northern India.
