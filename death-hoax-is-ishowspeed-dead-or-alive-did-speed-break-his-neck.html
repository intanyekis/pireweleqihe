    Death Hoax – Is Ishowspeed Dead or Alive?

    Opposite to the death hoax, Ishowspeed is not dead, but alive.

    There are rumours going around that Ishowpseed might have died after his recent trampoline incident. After he fell from the trampoline, netizens have assumed that he might have fatally broken his neck.

    However, this is not true. No official source has confirmed that Ishowspeed is dead. Furthermore, he did seem in conscious condition despite falling from the trampoline.

    This is not the first time, the YouTuber has been under a death conspiracy. A few months ago, someone played a prank on the streamer to deem him as lifeless.

    We can confirm he is alive, but not in good condition.

    Did Ishowspeed Break His Neck?

    Ishowspeed might have broken his neck.

    At first, everything was fine. The YouTuber was jumping higher and higher, in what seemed like a small trampoline. However, Speed suddenly landed his head on the edge of the trampoline.

    People watching the live stream could also hear crack noise, which may have been produced by the trampoline. Nevertheless, Ishowspeed was visibly in a lot of pain.

    Many people later slowed the video and speculated that Darren might have hurt his jaw. However, no update has been provided on Ishowspeed’s health status at the moment.
