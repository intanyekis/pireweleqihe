    The Mission: Impossible film franchise began in 1996, and stars famed actor Thomas Cruise Mapother IV – mostly known as Tom Cruise; it is based on Bruce Geller's 1966 television series.

    Since its inception, the series has featured a veritable constellation of big-name stars. The films are recognized for their jaw-dropping stunts, adventures, compelling plot lines, and ever-present espionage-related twists and turns.

    The Mission: Impossible franchise has accumulated over $4 billion over the last three decades with a production budget of $1.12 billion and includes seven total movies, making it the seventeenth highest-grossing franchise of all time (as of January 2024).

    Let's look at the significant actors who helped make the Mission: Impossible franchise so popular, the parts they played, and their estimated net worths.

    10 Rebecca Ferguson (Ilsa Faust)

    Rebecca Ferguson's Net Worth (Estimated): $6 Million

    Rebecca Ferguson, a 40-year-old Swedish actress, rose to international prominence in 2013 when she played Elizabeth Woodville in the British film The White Queen.

    Still, her breakthrough came in 2015 when she appeared as Ilsa Faust, an MI6 operative, in Mission: Impossible - Rogue Nation.

    Rebecca gained critical acclaim, became sought after, and has subsequently appeared in films such as Florence Foster Jenkins, The Girl On The Train, and The Greatest Showman.

    In her 24 years as an actor, she has been nominated for 21 awards and has won six.

    Married with two children, Rebecca Ferguson has a net worth estimated at $6 million.

    9 Michelle Monaghan (Julia Meade)

    Michelle Monaghan's Net Worth (Estimated): $13 Million

    Michelle Monaghan, 47, made her television debut in 2001 with an appearance onYoung Americans, which aired that year.

    However, her character as Julia Meade-Hunt, Ethan Hunt's fiancée and wife in the Mission: Impossible franchise, is her most fondly remembered.

    Monaghan shows us the character's vulnerable side, while simultaneously displaying a resilience everyone admires. She went on to create a promising acting career while holding down the fort as a wife and mother.

    The actress is married with two children, and she received a Golden Globe nomination for "Best Supporting Actress" in 2014. Gone, Baby, Gone, Eagle Eye, Maid of Honor, and The Heartbreak Kid are a few of her outstanding works.

    As of early 2024, Michelle Monaghan's net worth is estimated in the range of $13 million.

    8 Simon Pegg (Benji Dunn)

    Simon Pegg's Net Worth (Estimated): Between $25 Million And $30 Million

    British actor Simon Pegg, 53, plays the humorous and dependable character Benji in the Mission: Impossible franchise.

    He made his franchise debut as a lab technician in Mission: Impossible III in 2006. His character evolved over time, and he became one of Ethan's buddies and a field agent.

    However, it was not this part that made him famous. He is best known as the creator of the sitcom Spaced, which aired on Channel 4 in the United Kingdom.

    He's also recognized for his parts in Doctor Who, Star Trek, and Star Wars – regarded as one of the few performers to have attained the "holy grail of nerd-dom" – as well as for his involvement with Shaun Of The Dead, Hot Fuzz, and The World's End.

    Simon Pegg's net worth is estimated at between $25 million and $30 million (in American currency), and he is married with one child.

    7 Ving Rhames (Luther Stickell)

    Ving Rhames' Net Worth (Estimated): $25 Million

    Ving Rhames, who plays Ethan Hunt's best friend and hacker, has become a familiar face in the Mission: Impossible franchise; his role is one of assistance, dependability, and trust.

    Rhames plays the friend you'd call at 2 a.m., certain he will come through for you. The actor contributed to the franchise's familiarity as it evolved.

    Aside from Mission: Impossible, he is best known for his performance in the 1994 film Pulp Fiction, as well as other films such as E.R., Con Air, Dangerous Ground, and Out Of Sight.

    In his 39-year acting career, he has received three accolades, including the Golden Globe Award for Best Actor in 1998. The actor is currently married and has three children.

    As of early 2024, Ving Rhames' net worth is estimated at around $25 million.

    6 Philip Seymour Hoffman (Owen Davian)

    Philip Seymour Hoffman's Net Worth (Estimated At The Time Of His Death): $35 Million

    The late Philip Seymour Hoffman played deadly arms dealer Owen Davian, before his sudden death in 2014.

    Hoffman's character creates intrigue and tension in the film, and is remembered as one of Ethan Hunt's most memorable antagonists. He tested Ethan's resolve with psychological manipulations and calculated resolve.

    Several years after his death, Hoffman was named in 2022 as one of the 50 best actors of all time in an Empire poll. His major works include The Hunger Games, Doubt, The Master, The Big Lebowski, Magnolia, Happiness, Scent Of A Woman, and Synecdoche.

    Hoffman's talent made him an adored and respected actor, from his Oscar-winning performance in Capote to his scene-stealing turns in films such as The Big Lebowski and Boogie Nights.

    At the time of his death, Phillip Seymour Hoffman's net worth – calculated as part of the value of his estate – was around $35 million.

    5 Henry Cavill (August Walker)

    ​​​​Henry Cavill's Net Worth (Estimated): $50 Million

    We can all agree that Henry William Dalgliesh Cavill (Henry Cavill) is nothing short of a magnificent actor, best known for his role as Superman in the D.C. Universe.

    In 2015, Henry gained fame for his role as Superman in Superman: Man Of Steel. Since then, he has appeared in several major projects like The Witcher, in which he portrayed Geralt of Rivia, and Enola Holmes, in which he played Sherlock Holmes.

    In 2018, he played August Walker, a C.I.A. assassin at ease with killing anyone (including women and children) in the Mission: Impossible franchise.

    This character was very different from anything we had seen Cavill do before. In his 22 years as an actor, he has been nominated for 16 awards and won two.

    As of 2024, Henry Cavill's net worth stands at an estimated $50 million.

    4 Jon Voight (Jim Phelps)

    Jon Voight's Net Worth (Estimated): Between $55 Million And $60 Million

    Jon Voight appeared in the first installment of what would become a global franchise, as the leader of the Impossible Mission Force.

    Voight's Jim Phelps served as Ethan's tutor, setting the tone for the morals compass espoused by the I.M.F. He lent balance and mystery to the film, setting the tone for the espionage-filled franchise.

    Voight is well-known for several of his more notable works, like Midnight Cowboy, where he played John Buck, Deliverance, and Coming Home.

    He has received several accolades for his work, including an Academy Award, a BAFTA Award, four Golden Globe Awards, and four Primetime Emmy nominations. He received the National Medal of Arts in 2019.

    As of 2024, Jon Voight's net worth is estimated at between $55 million and $60 million.

    3 Alec Baldwin (Alan Hunley)

    Alec Baldwin's Net Worth (Estimated): Between $65 Million And $70 Million

    Long Island, New York-born actor Alec Baldwin played a significant character in Mission: Impossible – Rogue Nation and its sequel, Fallout.

    Baldwin portrayed Alan Hunley, a former director of the Criminal Intelligence Agency, who despised the Impossible Mission Force but eventually became its secretary after the I.M.F. beat a criminal organization called the Syndicate.

    Alan Walker, the C.I.A. assassin in Fallout, assassinated his character. Baldwin's character in Mission: Impossible is a testament to the actor's professionalism and versatility, as we watch him transform from a comedic actor to an authoritative one.

    Alec Baldwin's net worth is estimated to stand between $65 million and $70 million as of early 2024.

    2 Jeremy Renner (William Brandt)

    Jeremy Renner's Net Worth (Estimated): $80 Million

    Jeremy Renner is an American actor well known for his appearances in dark movies like Dahmer (2002), Hansel in Hansel And Gretel: Witch Hunters (2013) and in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU).

    Unsurprisingly, he made his first appearance in the Mission: Impossible franchise as William, a mysterious I.M.F. agent in a love-hate relationship with Ethan Hunt. His character complemented Ethan's reckless and daring actions as he was more level-headed and rule-abiding.

    Some of his other notable works include his work as Hawkeye in Avengers (2012, and subsequent MCU films), S.W.A.T. (2003), and 28 Weeks Later (2007).

    He has been nominated for two Academy Awards, and a Screen Actors Guild award for "Outstanding Cast" in 2014.

    Jeremy Renner's net worth is estimated consistenly at $80 million in early 2024.

    1 Tom Cruise (Ethan Hunt)

    Tom Cruise's Net Worth (Estimated): $600 Million

    Without Ethan Hunt, what is the Mission: Impossible franchise?

    Ethan, an I.M.F. field agent who is intensely loyal to his companions and will, under certain circumstances, breach all feasible rules to secure their safety, is played by Tom Cruise.

    His character is known for his death-defying antics, commitment to his goals, and belief in humanity. Cruise's insistence on performing his own stunts has earned him the respect of many colleagues and critics.

    He is widely regarded as the best stunt performer, second only to Jackie Chan. Cruise gave us films like Top Gun, The Firm, and Jerry Maguire; he has received over 140 accolades, and his films have grossed $11.5 billion worldwide.

    Tom Cruise is the highest paid actor in the Mission: Impossible franchise, and has an estimated net worth of $600 million, a testament not only to the popularity of the Mission: Impossible franchise but also to his 43-year filmography.
