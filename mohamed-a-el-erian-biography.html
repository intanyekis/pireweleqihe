    Who is Mohamed A. El-Erian?

    Mohamed A. El-Erian is an American entrepreneur and author. He is known for serving a management investment firm, PIMCO as its CEO.

    Moreover, he wrote books titled The Only Game in Town in 2016, When Markets Collide in 2008, and more.

    Mohamed A. El-Erian- Age, Parents, Ethnicity, Education

    His birthdate is 09 Aug 1958 and he was born in New York, New York, the United States as Mohamed Aly El-Erian. The name of his father and his mother is Abdullah El-Erian and Nadia Shoukry respectively.

    Mohamed’s ancestry is Egyptian and as per his academics, he passed from St John’s School.

    Also, he went to Queens College and achieved his bachelor’s degree in economics. He then enrolled at St Antony’s College to pursue his master’s degree plus a doctorate in economics

    Mohamed A. El-Erian- Professional Life, Career

    Mohamed A. El-Erian first joined International Monetary Fund in 1983 in 1997. Then he was appointed as Head of Emerging Markets by Salomon Smith Barney in 1988.

    After leaving this company in 1999, he became the MD of PIMCO in 1999. He was appointed as CEO in 2007 and worked for 7 years till 2014.

    At present, he is serving as President of Queens​ College, Senior Global Fellow at the University of Pennsylvania, and Advisor at GIC. His other work experiences are,

    • Professor of Practice at The Wharton School joined in Jul 2019.
    • Contributing Editor as Financial Times joined in March 2014.
    • Chair, Investment Advisory Committee at Microsoft joined in 2007.
    • President and CEO of Harvard Management from Feb 2006 till Dec 2007.
