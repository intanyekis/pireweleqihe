    Highlights and analysis of Morocco and Croatia's scoreless draw in Group F at World Cup 2022

    Croatia, who finished as 2018 World Cup runners-up, open this year's tournament up against Morocco. This game gives you another chance to see the majestic Luka Modric in action in what is likely to be his last World Cup at the age of 37.

    29 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Croatia, who finished as 2018 World Cup runners-up, open this year's tournament up against Morocco.

    This game gives you another chance to see the majestic Luka Modric in action in what is likely to be his last World Cup at the age of 37.

    Morocco have the likes of Hakim Ziyech and Achraf Hakimi at their disposal.

    The two sides are joined in Group F with Belgium and Canada.

    Team news

    Morocco: Yassine Bounou, Achraf Hakimi, Noussair Mazraoui, Sofyan Amrabat, Nayef Aguerd, Romain Saiss, Hakim Ziyech, Azzedine Ounahi, Selim Amallah, Soufiane Boufal, Youssef En-Nesyri.

    Croatia: Dominik Livakovic, Ivan Perisic, Dejan Lovren, Mateo Kovacic, Andrej Kramaric, Luka Modric, Marcelo Brozovic, Nikola Vlasic, Borna Sosa, Josko Gvardiol, Josip Juranovic.
