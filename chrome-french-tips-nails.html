    With fall right around the corner, it’s time to make a trip to your go-to nail salon for a quick manicure fix that will help you embrace the season to the fullest. And the manicure that everyone’s obsessing about right now is Chrome French tips. Something that an ambivert would absolutely love, chrome french tips are simple and clean, but always up for a good time.

    The current ‘hot girl’ staple, this manicure is fun, chic, and perfect for everyone who loves to keep it simple. Tempted to try it out? Here are some of our versions of the trend du jour.

    Chrome Claws

    Classic, reflective silver chrome can never go out of style, especially when it’s done on mountain peak-shaped nails. Looks like it’s time to bring out your claws!

    Berry Tips

    It’s always a good idea for pink nails, especially if they are in chrome! Add some colour to your tips with these berry tones that can brighten up your entire look.

    Pearly Silvers

    Want to keep it simple but still go over the top? Just add some pearls! Jazz up your silver tips with some tiny pearls to make the look even more fun.

    Chrome Squares

    Chrome tips look good on all shapes and sizes, especially on squares! Just remember, keep them super thin for maximum impact.

    Gold Rimmed

    Simple and classic, if you prefer to opt for a manicure that is clean and minimal, these gold french tips might just be what you need in your life.

    Evil Eye

    Want to cast off an evil eye? What better way to do it than through your nails? Just add a classic blue evil eye to up your chrome game.
