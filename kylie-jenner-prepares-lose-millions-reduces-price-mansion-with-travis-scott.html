    Kylie Jenner Prepares To Lose Millions As She Reduces Price On Mansion With Travis Scott

    Its been around two years since Kylie Jenner and Travis Scott called it quits, but the former couple are still desperately trying to sell the house they once shared together. In a bid to increase interest, the parents-of-two have reportedly reduced the asking price on the million-dollar mansion again.

    18 Jun 2024, 00:00

    Highlights

    • Kylie Jenner and Travis Scott are still struggling to sell their shared Beverly Hills mansion even after reducing the price by 10%.
    • The couple first bought the property for $13.4 million in 2018 and are now trying to sell it for just under $18 million.
    • Despite its luxury features, sentimental value, and being the first home they shared, Kylie and Travis are eager to part ways with the property.

    It’s been around two years since Kylie Jenner and Travis Scott called it quits, but the former couple are still desperately trying to sell the house they once shared together. In a bid to increase interest, the parents-of-two have reportedly reduced the asking price on the million-dollar mansion again.

    According to TMZ, the former couple have once again placed their Beverly Hills residence on the market, but with a significant reduction. They first acquired the home back in 2018 after their daughter, Stormi, was born for $13.4 million. Now, they’re hoping to offload the property for just under $18 million.

    This is a substantial decrease from their earlier price when they initially attempted to sell the property back in 2022. At the time, they set an asking price of $21.9 million, though they’ve made adjustments to the price in the years that follow, though it’s yet to result in a sale. The current adjustment reflects a 10% markdown, proving Kylie and Travis aren’t afraid to forgo millions in profits if it means finally saying goodbye to what was once their joint home.

    Despite the adjustment, the Beverly Hills home still carries a high price tag, which his explained by the various luxury features Travis and Kylie enjoyed together for a time.

    The residence has 7 bedrooms and 11 baths, comprising over 9,000 square feet of living space. It also boasts an array of amenities, including a private movie theatre, a gym, a massage room, and a pool.

    Kylie Jenner

    It's worth noting that this property holds sentimental value as it marks the first home shared by Kylie Jenner and Travis Scott. However, following their separation, the necessity for this extravagant residence in their lives has understandably diminished. Despite its lavish features and ample space, circumstances have led them to part ways with the property, paving the way for new occupants to enjoy its luxurious offerings.

    Kylie – who has been dating actor Timothee Chalamet since last year, despite recent reports of a potential break-up – shares a son and daughter with Travis. The couple split after several years of being on-and-off in late 2022, though it wasn’t confirmed until the start of 2023. Kylie went public with her relationship with Timothee later that summer.

    The makeup mogul recently finished construction on her mega-mansion, which makes her neighbours with mom Kris Jenner and sister Khloe Kardashian. The custom property is likely the most expensive in her portfolio, and despite having a net worth of nearly $1 billion thanks to her social media influence and business ventures, it may explain why Kylie is so eager to offload the home she once shared with Travis.
