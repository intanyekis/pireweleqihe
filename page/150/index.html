Jim Salisbury of NBC Sports Philadelphia confirmed the deal, while noting that Realmuto now holds the largest average annual value ever for a catcher ($23.1 million). It's also the third richest contract ever handed out to a catcher. The record is currently held by former Minnesota Twins All-Star Joe Mauer, who signed an eight-year extension worth $184 million in 2010.
