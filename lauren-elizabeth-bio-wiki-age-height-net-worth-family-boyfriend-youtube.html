    Lauren Elizabeth YouTuber

    Lauren began her career as a blogger writing about fashion and beauty. She later opened her YouTube channel where she posted small clips as she shared her tips on fashion and beauty. Her fanbase grew slowly and that’s when she began to create relatable content for women. She inspires women to curate their best lives and embrace their best selves. She opened her podcast called MOOD, where she discusses mental health, wellness, and lifestyle. She also runs her clothing brand called Lauren Elizabeth Collection.
