    Chelsey Crisp Wiki Biography

    Chelsey Crisp was born in 1983, in Phoenix, Arizona USA, and is a writer, comedian and actress, best known for being part of the television sitcom “Fresh off the Boat”, in which she plays the role of Honey Ellis. She’s been active in the industry since 2004, and all of her endeavors have helped put her net worth to where it is today.

    How rich is Chelsey Crisp? As of late-2017, sources estimate a net worth that is at $1.5 million, mostly earned through success in acting and in comedy, having appeared in numerous films, television shows, and theatre productions. She also does improv and sketch comedy. As she continues her career, it is expected that her wealth will also continue to increase.

    Chelsey Crisp Net Worth $1.5 million

    Growing up, Chelsey developed an interest for acting, and would start to perform in community theater at a young age. She attended Agua Fria High School, and after matriculating would then go to the American Academy of Dramatic Arts from 2001 to 2003. After graduating, she would then take further studies as part of the British American Dramatic Academy’s Midsummer in Oxford Shakespeare program.

    In 2004, Crisp made her television debut as part of “Scare Tactics”; the series is a prank horror hidden camera show which puts their victims into horror movie cliché situations, thanks to the help of makeup and special effects. Often times it is the victim’s family or friends that set the prank up. She then got her first film role in “Dr. Chopper”, a horror film about friends who encounter a motorcycle-riding serial killer. The film would open up more opportunities for her to increase her net worth, and she appeared in various television shows, mainly in a minor or guest roles in such as “New Girl”, “Happy Endings”, “CSI: Miami” and “The Office”. She also became a star of the mock reality game show entitled “The Joe Schmo Show”, in which she played the model Chloe. The premise of the series is that they target people into believing they are contestants of a reality show, when they are actually not.

    Chelsey’s net worth has continued to increase with more projects over the years. She became a member of the comedy team called Duchess Riot in 2011, which she would also serve as their artistic director; the group focuses on sketch comedy and improv. She has also appeared in more films, such as “Reconciliation”, “Bleed” and “In-Lawfully Yours” which was released in 2016 also starring Corbin Bernsen and Marilu Henner. A few of Chelsey’s latest projects include the comedy series “Fresh off the Boat” which is inspired by the life of food personality Eddie Huang; she appears in the series as the family neighbor Honey.

    For her personal life, it is known that Crisp married screenwriter Rheet Reese in 2016; he’s best known for writing for films such as “Deadpool” and “Zombieland”. They reside in Los Angeles, California.
